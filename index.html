About

Improving the diagnostics of typhoid

Typhoid or Enteric fever is one of the infectious human diseases in Cameroon and Africa. Outbreaks of typhoid fever caused by Salmonella typhi remains a serious health problem worldwide.

There are a number of tests available presently, from molecular to immunological and biochemical to microbiological. Some well-known Method of Diagnosis of Typhoid Fever are : Microbial Cultures and Antibody detection tests:

Both have proven to not be 100% reliable as the use of Microbial Cultures is very expensive and difficult and the Antibody detection test revealed poor reliability, low sensitivity and specificity.

The goal of this project is to improve the diagnosis of Typhoid disease through the use of Augmented Intelligence.
